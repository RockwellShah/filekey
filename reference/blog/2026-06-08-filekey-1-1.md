FileKey 1.1 brings the app to Safari and iPhone, makes large files painless, and polishes sharing, contacts, and recovery throughout.

## What's new

- **Safari and iPhone support.** FileKey now works in Safari, including on iPhone, alongside every other modern browser. The whole flow, from enrolling a passkey to decrypting a file, works on the device in your pocket.
- **Smooth large files.** Large files now encrypt and decrypt off the main thread, so the page stays responsive and never freezes, no matter the file size.
- **Clearer sharing, contacts, and recovery.** The sharing flow, your contacts list, and the recovery steps all got refreshed copy and clearer layouts, so it's obvious what's happening at each step.

As always, nothing about this changed the fundamentals: no accounts, no server, and nothing leaves your device. You can read [the exact source](/source.txt) that runs in your browser.
